# Privacy Policy for BreakRot

**Last Updated:** January 16, 2026

## Introduction

BreakRot ("we," "our," or "us") is a digital wellness application designed to help you manage your screen time and build healthier app usage habits. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Usage Statistics Data
We access Android's Usage Stats API to collect information about your app usage patterns. This includes:
- Names and package identifiers of installed applications
- Time spent in each application
- Application launch frequencies

**Important:** This data is processed and stored **locally on your device only**. We do not transmit usage statistics data to any external servers.

### 2. User Preferences
We store your preferences locally on your device, including:
- Daily screen time limits you set
- Apps you choose to monitor
- Focus session settings and history
- Onboarding completion status

### 3. Cloud Data (Firebase Firestore)
If you are signed in with a Google account, certain data may be synced to Firebase Firestore for backup and cross-device access:
- Monitored apps and their time limits
- Personal goals
- Feedback submissions

This data is stored securely in Google Cloud and is associated with your user account. You can delete this data by removing monitored apps or goals within the app.

### 4. Analytics Data (Firebase)
We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data to improve our application. This may include:
- App performance metrics
- Crash reports
- General usage patterns (e.g., feature usage frequency)

This data is anonymized and does not contain personally identifiable information.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
1. Display your screen time statistics
2. Send you break reminders when you exceed your set limits
3. Track your focus session history
4. Improve application performance and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
Screen time statistics and daily usage data are processed and stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences and DataStore.

### Cloud Storage (Firestore)
If signed in, the following data is synced to Firebase Firestore:
- Monitored apps configuration
- Personal goals
- Help/feedback submissions

Data in Firestore is protected by Firebase security rules and is only accessible to authenticated users.

### Third-Party Services
We use Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics). Firebase's data handling is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

## Permissions We Request

### Usage Access Permission
Required to read app usage statistics. This is essential for the core functionality of tracking screen time.

### Notification Permission
Required to send you break reminders and focus session notifications.

### Overlay Permission (if applicable)
May be requested to display break reminder dialogs over other apps.

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Stored on your device until you uninstall the application or clear app data.
- **Analytics Data:** Retained according to Firebase's data retention policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all your usage data within the app.
- **Delete:** Clear all local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through Android settings.
- **Control:** Enable or disable notifications and modify monitoring preferences at any time.

## Children's Privacy

BreakRot is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
